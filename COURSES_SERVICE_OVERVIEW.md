# Courses Service Overview

This document describes the course-focused services we provide to learners and clients.

---
## Course Catalog

- A structured catalog of courses with clear titles and descriptions.
- Courses are grouped by category to improve discovery.
- Each course has a unique identifier for reliable tracking.

---
## Course Details & Outcomes

- Clear information about what students will learn.
- Highlights level, duration, and expected outcomes.
- Helps learners choose the right course with confidence.

---
## Availability & Status

- Courses can be marked active, inactive, or upcoming.
- Keeps the catalog clean and focused on current offerings.

---
## Learner Experience Benefits

- Faster discovery through organized categories.
- Better decision-making with transparent course details.
- Reliable enrollment and support due to consistent course data.
